Program Meaning and Significance

The Kids and PLAY program is modeled after the GRASSROOTS development of young children through active participation in a sporting activity (ATHLETICS), which will allow for maximum spread of acceptability because of its variety of activities and its possibility to be engaged by children regardless of their economic status, weight, height, special needs, handicapped, and lack of skillsâ€¦ Athletics accepts all comers and grooms them to the best of their natural abilities and commitments to the endeavor

Program Benefits

  • Promoting health and wellness of school aged children
  • Inculcating fitness and athletic excellence in every active participant
  • Teaching and learning of discipline, focus, time management, team work, leadership and selfdrive early in life as a young child in a well nurturing environment
  • Meeting and working with other kids who will grow to become lifelong friends
  • Staying away from crime, drugs and restive situations
  • Growing in an environment that has built in support systems to ensure success in life
