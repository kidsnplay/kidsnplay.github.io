About Us

The “Kids and play” initiative is an Non Governmental Organization set at supporting child development through physical fitness(sports) and arts thereby ......the component of education for the Nigerian child by providing opportunities for students/kids to actively engage recreationally. This contributes to the holistic development of young people through participation where learn about the importance of key values such as: honesty, team work, fair play, respect for themselves as well as others and adherence to rules.
