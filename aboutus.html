Kids and Play was initiated in 2014, with the focus set at supporting child development through physical fitness(sports) and arts thereby providing a platform to add the component of education for the Nigerian child by providing opportunities for students/kids to actively engage recreationally. Kids and PLAY is also set in remembrance of JANET NKECHI MGBEKEENAHORO, the mother of the founder, Dr. Michael Enahoro. She inspired his sporting career, and that of Ituah Enahoro, his son, who is also an Athlete. By 2015, there were over 450 kids registered in the program, and participating in Athletics across 6 (Six) centres across the FCT. Some of the achievements, Kids and PLAY gave the world, ROSEMARY CHUKWUMA, the ex-Nigeria, Africa, World and Olympic YOUTH Champion Sprinter in the 100m, 200m, and she is a Commonwealth Bronze medalist. We have over 10 graduates of the program in various national athletics teams and are collegiate athletes in both Nigeria and United States and the UK.

Kids and Play is a component of education for the Nigerian child by providing opportunities for students/kids to actively engage recreationally. This contributes to the holistic development of young people through participation where learn about the importance of key values such as: honesty, team work, fair play, respect for themselves as well as others and adherence to rules.
